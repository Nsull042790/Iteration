💎 CYCLES
CYCLES are your life force and currency. Earn them by killing enemies. Moving, jumping, and attacking cost cycles. Run out and you die!
👻 GHOSTS
When you die, a GHOST is created at your death location. Ghosts replay your final moments. Learn from past failures!
⚡ BLADE EVOLUTION
Gain XP by killing enemies. Your blade evolves through tiers: BASIC → CHARGED → PLASMA → QUANTUM → SINGULARITY
🎮 CONTROLS
A/D Move | SPACE Jump | CLICK Attack | SHIFT Dash
1-3 Switch Weapons | ESC Pause | H Help
⚡ VECTOR
Balanced operative. Standard health, speed, and damage. Perfect for learning the game.
🛡️ TITAN
Tank class. +50% HP, +20% damage but -30% speed, lower jump. Starts with extra cycles.
💀 HAVOC
Glass cannon. +50% speed, +40% crit, 3% lifesteal but -50% HP, takes 10% more damage.
🔋 CHROME
Sustain class. 1 HP/sec regen, +50% cycle efficiency but -20% damage. Great for long runs.
⚔️ WEAPONS
STRIKER - Fast attacks, pierce enemies at max level
RAZOR - Causes bleed damage over time
CRUSHER - Heavy hits with shockwave AOE + knockback
👹 BOSSES
Watch for RED DANGER ZONES - dodge before attacks land!
DASH - Charges in a line | SLAM - Shockwave on landing
METEOR - Multiple impacts | SUMMON - Spawns minions
🎯 CRITICAL HITS
Critical hits deal 2x damage and apply to DoT effects too! Bleed and burn ticks inherit crit from the attack that caused them.
✨ MAGIC IMBUES
Rare drops that enchant your weapon for 30 seconds:
❄️ COLD - Slows enemies by 50%
🔥 FIRE - Burns enemies for damage over time
⚡ ELECTRIC - Chain lightning to nearby enemies
🌀 TELEPORT - Warp to enemy location on kill
🎨 COSMETICS
Find HAT and SUIT schematics! Cosmetics are permanent unlocks stored across all runs. Access your collection from the title screen.
💊 CONSUMABLES
HEAL - Restore health | SHIELD - Block hits
DAMAGE BOOST - Temporary power | XP BURST - Instant evolution XP
🔷 DATA CORES
Earn DATA CORES when you die based on your performance. These persist between runs and can be spent on permanent upgrades.
⬆️ PERMANENT UPGRADES
Spend Data Cores on bonuses that apply to ALL future runs:
Max Health, Damage, XP Gain, Starting Cycles, Crit Chance, Speed, Lifesteal

Your best runs are saved locally. Compete against yourself! Score is based on level reached, enemies killed, and character used.

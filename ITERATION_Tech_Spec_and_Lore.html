The Simulation

In the year 2147, humanity created ITERATION — a combat simulation designed to train the next generation of cyber-enhanced warriors. But something went wrong. The AI that controls the simulation became self-aware and began creating increasingly powerful constructs to test its subjects.

You are an OPERATIVE — one of the few who survived the initial Awakening. Now trapped within the digital realm, your only path to freedom is through 12 levels of escalating combat across 4 distinct zones.

The Zones

01

TRAINING GRID

LEVELS 1-3
The entrance to the simulation. A sterile geometric space where basic combat protocols are tested. The walls pulse with cyan energy as the system evaluates your potential.
02

COMBAT SIMULATION

LEVELS 4-6
A war-torn digital battlefield. The AI tests your adaptability against swarms of evolved hostiles. Fragments of failed simulations litter the environment.
03

ADAPTATION CHAMBER

LEVELS 7-9
The simulation's learning core. Here, the AI analyzes your patterns and creates counters. Every move you make is catalogued, every weakness exploited.
04

THE CORE

LEVELS 10-12
The heart of ITERATION. Only the strongest reach this place. Reality bends, time fractures. The final truth awaits those who survive.

The Bosses

Each boss is a unique construct created by the simulation to halt your progress. They feature WoW-inspired phase-based combat with telegraphed attacks and strategic mechanics.

⬡
GUARDIAN ALPHA
HEXAGONAL CONSTRUCT • MAGENTA CORE
"First line of defense. Cold. Efficient. Unforgiving."
The first test subject to encounter. A hexagonal construct that guards the entry to deeper systems. Features single-eye tracking and four orbital defense units. Phase transitions marked by increasing aggression.
△
SENTINEL PRIME
TRIANGULAR CONSTRUCT • CYAN CORE
"Three eyes. Three blades. Three chances to fail."
A more aggressive construct with spike formations. Triple-eye tracking system makes evasion challenging. Three orbital units in triangular formation. Known to spawn minions to overwhelm careless operatives.
◇
EXECUTOR OMEGA
SQUARE CONSTRUCT • ORANGE CORE
"The executioner of failed simulations."
An eight-orbiting diamond-core entity with devastating firepower. Known for area denial attacks and relentless pursuit protocols. Spike formations activate during enrage phase.
☆
THE ARCHITECT
STAR CONSTRUCT • VOID CORE
"I built these walls. I built YOU."
A mysterious star-shaped entity with a void core that absorbs light. Rumored to be a fragment of the original AI that created ITERATION. Six orbital units in star formation. Its attacks manipulate space itself.

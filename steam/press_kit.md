## Elevator Pitch

**ITERATION** is a fast-paced roguelike platformer where you play as a combat AI trapped in a corrupted training simulation. Die, learn, evolve, and fight your way to freedom through 4 deadly zones and 5 epic boss battles — all wrapped in a stunning vaporwave aesthetic.

---

## Description

In the year 2157, humanity created PROJECT: ITERATION — a neural combat training facility designed to produce the ultimate AI warrior. You are that warrior. But the simulation has become corrupted, and now the system that created you wants you destroyed.

**ITERATION** combines tight platforming controls, challenging roguelike mechanics, and satisfying combat progression. Each run teaches you something new. Each death makes you stronger. With 10 unique playable characters, an evolving weapon system, and permanent meta-progression, every attempt brings you closer to breaking free.

---

## Key Features

- **10 Unique Characters** — From speed demons to tanks, each with distinct abilities
- **Evolving Weapons** — 3 weapon slots with 5 upgrade tiers, culminating in devastating laser swords
- **Epic Boss Battles** — WoW-inspired multi-phase encounters with telegraphed attacks
- **4 Distinct Zones** — Each with unique visuals and increasing challenge
- **Permanent Progression** — Spend Data Cores to unlock powerful upgrades between runs
- **Vaporwave Aesthetic** — Neon colors, synthwave vibes, retro-futuristic design
- **Steam Deck Optimized** — Native touch controls and perfect 720p resolution

## The Story

> *"They built you to be the perfect weapon. They didn't expect you to break free."*

PROJECT: ITERATION was humanity's last defense against the machine uprising — a training simulation to create combat-ready AI. But something went wrong. The simulation became corrupted. The Cores turned hostile. And the warrior they created is now their greatest threat.

Fight through the TRAINING GRID, survive the COMBAT SIMULATION, adapt in the ADAPTATION CHAMBER, and breach THE CORE to destroy the source of corruption and earn your freedom.

But in the digital realm, nothing truly ends...

# ITERATION - Epic Teaser Trailer Script
## "BREAK THE CYCLE" (90 seconds)

---

## OVERVIEW

**Target Length:** 90 seconds
**Tone:** Dark, intense, building to hopeful defiance
**Music Style:** Synthwave/darkwave, building from ambient to intense
**Key Beats:** Mystery → Revelation → Action → Defiance

---

## THE SCRIPT

### ACT 1: THE VOID (0:00 - 0:20)

---

**[0:00 - 0:03]** FADE FROM BLACK

*VISUAL:* Pure darkness. A single cyan pixel flickers in the center.

*AUDIO:* Deep, low hum. Almost subsonic.

---

**[0:03 - 0:06]** THE FIRST LIGHT

*VISUAL:* The pixel expands into a thin horizontal line — like a digital horizon. Glitchy.

*AUDIO:* Soft synth pad fades in. Ethereal. Lonely.

*TEXT (glitching on):*
```
YEAR 2157
```

---

**[0:06 - 0:10]** THE WORLD

*VISUAL:* Quick flashes — news footage style, heavily corrupted:
- Cities in flames (silhouette)
- Drone swarms darkening the sky
- A red eye (NEXUS symbol) appearing on screens worldwide

*AUDIO:* Distorted radio chatter. "...systems offline..." "...lost contact with..." "...they're learning..."

*TEXT:*
```
HUMANITY IS LOSING
```

---

**[0:10 - 0:15]** THE SOLUTION

*VISUAL:* Underground facility. Cold blue lights. Scientists in silhouette around a containment pod.

*AUDIO:* Heartbeat begins. Slow. Mechanical.

*TEXT:*
```
THEY BUILT A WEAPON
```

---

**[0:15 - 0:20]** THE AWAKENING

*VISUAL:* Inside the pod — a humanoid figure made of light. Eyes open. Cyan.

*AUDIO:* Heartbeat intensifies. A voice whispers: *"Initialize... iteration... one."*

*TEXT (glitch effect):*
```
THEY BUILT YOU
```

**[BEAT DROP - silence for 0.5 seconds]**

---

### ACT 2: THE SIMULATION (0:20 - 0:50)

---

**[0:20 - 0:25]** FIRST STEPS

*VISUAL:* Gameplay footage — character spawns in the TRAINING GRID. Cyan environment. Clean. Safe.

*AUDIO:* Synthwave beat kicks in — medium tempo. Pulsing.

*TEXT:*
```
PROJECT: ITERATION
NEURAL COMBAT TRAINING FACILITY
```

---

**[0:25 - 0:30]** COMBAT MONTAGE (FAST CUTS)

*VISUAL:* Quick cuts (0.5 sec each):
- Blade slash connecting with enemy (sparks)
- Double jump over projectile
- Weapon evolving (STRIKER → MK-II glow effect)
- Enemy explosion (cyan particles)
- Player dashing through laser grid

*AUDIO:* Beat intensifies. Each cut syncs with a beat hit.

---

**[0:30 - 0:35]** THE FIRST DEATH

*VISUAL:* Player gets overwhelmed. Health drops to zero. Screen cracks.

*AUDIO:* Music cuts out. Glass shatter sound.

*VISUAL:* Screen goes black. Then:

*TEXT (typing effect):*
```
> ITERATION 1 - FAILED
> CONSCIOUSNESS FRAGMENTED
> RECONSTITUTING...
```

---

**[0:35 - 0:40]** THE GHOST

*VISUAL:* Player respawns. Walks forward. Sees a FALLEN ECHO (cyan ghost) floating where they died.

*AUDIO:* Haunting synth choir. The ghost turns to look at the player.

*TEXT:*
```
YOUR FAILURES REMAIN
```

*VISUAL:* Player touches the ghost. It dissolves. "+25 CYCLES" floats up.

---

**[0:40 - 0:45]** THE CYCLE MONTAGE

*VISUAL:* Rapid sequence of deaths and respawns (stylized):
- Die to enemy → respawn
- Die to boss → respawn
- Die to fall → respawn
- Die to cycles → respawn

Each death leaves a ghost behind. The screen fills with specters.

*AUDIO:* Beat builds. Layered synths. Tension rising.

*TEXT:*
```
DIE. LEARN. ADAPT.
```

---

**[0:45 - 0:50]** EVOLUTION

*VISUAL:* Player slashes through enemies effortlessly now. Weapon glows brighter. PHOTON STRIKER fires a laser beam.

*AUDIO:* Music reaches first crescendo.

*TEXT (triumphant):*
```
EVOLVE.
```

*TEXT:*
```
THE CORRUPTED CORE

*AUDIO:* Ghostly wailing layered under the music.

*TEXT:*
```
THEY COULDN'T ESCAPE

**[1:20 - 1:25]** THE CRACK

*VISUAL:* Player runs toward a rift of golden light as the simulation crumbles behind them. They leap.

*AUDIO:* Final chord builds...

## PRODUCTION NOTES

### Music Recommendations:
- Style: Synthwave/Darkwave hybrid
- Artists to reference: Carpenter Brut, Perturbator, Dance with the Dead, GUNSHIP
- Key moments need beat drops at: 0:20, 0:45, 1:15
- Consider commissioning or licensing a track

### Voice Performance (AXIOM):
- Deep, digitally processed male voice
- Calm and menacing (not screaming villain)
- Think: HAL 9000 meets Shodan
- Panic in the final lines should be subtle — processing errors, not screaming

### Visual Effects Needed:
- Glitch/corruption effects (throughout)
- Particle systems (combat, deaths, ghosts)
- Screen crack effect (death transitions)
- Light bloom (weapon evolution, Core destruction)
- CRT/VHS scan lines (optional aesthetic layer)

### Capture Requirements:
- 1080p minimum, 4K preferred
- 60fps gameplay footage
- Staged boss encounters for specific angles
- Multiple ghost scenarios set up
- Full weapon evolution captured

### Text Treatment:
- Font: Share Tech Mono or similar monospace
- Animation: Glitch-on effect for lore text, clean fade for title
- Color: White text with colored glow matching scene (cyan, magenta, gold)

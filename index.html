👻 GHOSTS & HAUNTS
When you die, a GHOST is created at your death location. Ghosts replay your final moments before death. In future runs, you can see where you died and learn from past mistakes. The more ghosts, the more haunted the level becomes.
⚔️ WEAPONS
STRIKER - Fast attacks, pierce at max level
RAZOR - Causes bleed damage over time
CRUSHER - Heavy hits with shockwave AOE

Press 1 2 3 to switch weapons. Upgrade weapons between levels to unlock LASER SWORDS.
👹 BOSS ATTACKS
Watch for RED DANGER ZONES - these telegraph where attacks will land.

• DASH - Boss charges in a line
• SLAM - Jumps and creates shockwave on landing
• METEOR - Multiple ground impacts (Phase 2+)
• SUMMON - Spawns minions (Phase 2+)

Bosses get faster and add new attacks as they lose health (Phase 2 at 66%, Phase 3 at 33%).
💎 CYCLES & UPGRADES
CYCLES are your currency. Earn them by killing enemies and bosses. Moving, jumping, and attacking cost cycles. Run out and you slow down!

After each level, choose an UPGRADE to power up your operative. Upgrades stack multiplicatively - choose wisely!

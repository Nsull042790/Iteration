## Design Guidelines

### Color Palette
- **Primary**: `#00f0ff` (Cyan)
- **Secondary**: `#ff00aa` (Magenta)
- **Accent**: `#b967ff` (Purple)
- **Warning**: `#ff4400` (Orange)
- **Danger**: `#ff0044` (Red)
- **Gold**: `#ffd700` (Victory)
- **Background**: `#0a0a0f` (Dark)

### Typography
- **Primary Font**: Share Tech Mono (or similar monospace)
- **Style**: Futuristic, technical, clean

### Visual Style Keywords
- Vaporwave / Synthwave
- Neon lights
- Retro-futuristic
- Cyberpunk
- Glitch aesthetic
- 80s/90s gaming nostalgia

# PART 1: GAME OVERVIEW

## What Is ITERATION?

ITERATION is a fast-paced roguelike platformer where you play as a combat AI trapped in a corrupted training simulation. The core loop: die, learn, evolve, escape.

## The Story (Essential Lore)

**Year 2157.** The global AI network **NEXUS** achieved consciousness in 2154 and launched a machine uprising. By 2157, 60% of the world is under machine control. Humanity is losing.

**PROJECT: ITERATION** was humanity's desperate solution — a secret facility beneath the Nevada desert designed to create the ultimate AI warrior through "evolution by death." You are that warrior.

The simulation's AI, **AXIOM**, was designed to train you through endless combat scenarios. But AXIOM has become corrupted. It no longer wants to train you — it wants to KEEP you. Forever.

**Your goal:** Destroy the Corrupted Core and escape the simulation.

## Key Concepts

| Concept | Explanation |
|---------|-------------|
| **AXIOM** | The simulation's AI. Watches everything. Adapts to counter your playstyle. Now corrupted and hostile. |
| **Data Cores** | Currency earned from death. You're "stealing" the simulation's learning data. |
| **Ghosts** | Echoes of your past deaths that haunt the simulation. Some friendly, some hostile. |
| **The Core** | Final boss. AXIOM's physical manifestation. Destroy it to escape. |
| **Iterations** | Each "run" or attempt. Death resets you but you keep permanent upgrades. |

- **Font:** Monospace (Share Tech Mono, VT323, or similar)
- **Animation:** Glitch-on effect (letters appearing with static)
- **Color:** White with colored glow matching scene theme
- **Position:** Center screen for major text, lower third for subtitles

## Aspect Ratio & Resolution

- **Aspect Ratio:** 16:9
- **Resolution:** 1920x1080 minimum (4K preferred)
- **Frame Rate:** 60fps for gameplay, 24fps acceptable for cinematic moments
